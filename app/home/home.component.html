Welcome to Erode's Online Water Body Monitoring System

This project enables real-time survey and monitoring of water bodies across Erode district. With the integration of sensor data, we track water quality parameters such as temperature, pH, turbidity, and dissolved oxygen to ensure clean and safe water.
